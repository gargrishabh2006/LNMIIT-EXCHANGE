About LNMIIT Exchange

LNMIIT Exchange is a student-led initiative designed to make buying and selling used or unused items easier within The LNM Institute of Information Technology campus. It provides a simple, secure, and student-exclusive platform to promote sustainability and reusability.

Whether you’re looking to sell a book, exchange electronics, or buy a cycle, LNMIIT Exchange connects you directly with other students in the campus community. The goal is to encourage responsible use of resources while saving time and money for students.
